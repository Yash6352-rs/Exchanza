Exchanza is a mobile platform where users exchange skills or services without money by 
creating “offer” and “request” posts. Users can browse a discovery feed, filter by interests, 
and send trade proposals to others. Once a proposal is accepted, a private chat opens to 
coordinate the exchange. After completing a trade, both users can rate each other and generate 
a downloadable PDF summary of the transaction. The app also supports notifications, user profiles, 
and offline functionality using local storage. An admin panel helps manage users, posts, and 
overall platform activity.
